Ingredients:
 - 2 tomatos
 - 2 eggs
 - 1/2 green onion
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 teaspoon sugar
 - 2 ml oil

Directions:
  1. Cut tomatos into pieces.
  2. Chop green onion into small segments (about 1/4 inches a piece).
  3. Break eggs into a bowl and mix.
  4. Put oil into the frying pan. Turn on heat.
  5. Pour mixed eggs into the frying pan and stir.
  6. When the eggs turn white a little, put the tomatos into the frying pan and stir.
  7. Put in green onions, salt and sugar. Stir.
  8. After the tomatos soften a bit, turn off heat. Dish up.
